## Skill: Market Readiness Checklist

**Trigger**: Before entering a new corridor or market.

1. Regulatory: drone permits, airspace restrictions, data privacy laws
2. Crops: what do farmers grow here? What are the top 5 problems?
3. Infrastructure: cellular coverage, road access, nearest charging station
4. Competition: who else operates here? What do they offer?
5. Pricing: what can local farmers afford? MXN/hectare/month benchmark
6. Partners: local agronomists, cooperatives, SAGARPA extension agents

## Skill: Localization Audit

**Trigger**: When adapting for a new region (e.g., Ontario).

1. Language: translate all farmer-facing content (Spanish → English for Ontario)
2. Units: hectares → acres (if needed), MXN → CAD
3. Crops: update disease library, seasonal calendars, treatment recommendations
4. Regulations: AFAC → Transport Canada drone rules, organic certification differences
5. Weather: update thresholds for local climate (Ontario winters vs Jalisco heat)
