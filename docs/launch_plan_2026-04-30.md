## Customer #1 path — funnel mechanics

```
30 trade-show qualified leads (Outdoor Farm Show booth scan)
   ↓ 27% (8 leads)
8 free 1-flight demos booked (within 30 days post-show)
   ↓ 50% (4 leads)
4 paid 1-season pilots @ Founding-100 rate ($48/ac × 250 ac avg = $12K)
   ↓ 90% (4 customers; assume all renew given founding lock)
4 annual customers entering 2027 at Standard $72/ac = $18K ARR each
```

Plus organic + radius marketing → 21 additional paying customers Y1 close = **25 total**.

**What if the funnel slips?**
- 50% leads → 15 demos: ARR target hit only if pilot conversion stays 50%+
- Pilot conversion floor: 30% — acceptable if avg-customer-acres skews bigger (>250 ac)
- Founding-100 lock = the safety net: even if Y1 raw revenue weak, 100 logos × Y2 case-study compounding = Series A trigger

## Customer-#1 scenarios (JPL's question)

**"What happens when 30 sign up but only pilot?"**

Pilot is the wedge — designed to convert, not loss-lead. Mechanics:

1. **Founding-100 pricing concession** ($48/ac vs Standard $72) only available to first-100 farms via trade-show channel. Locks logo density + case studies for Y2 sales motion. ROI of concession: $24/ac × 250 ac × 100 farms = $600K of "marketing budget" via discounted-revenue, vs $1.8M cash equivalent at full Standard rates.
2. **Pilot terms** = 1 season paid up-front (refundable on month-1 cancel), full Cerebro access, dedicated agronomist contractor hours, in-season case study development.
3. **Conversion math:** even at 50% pilot-to-annual conversion (worst case), 4 pilots → 2 annual @ $18K = $36K ARR + 2 referenceable case studies. At 90% conversion (target), 4 → 4 = $72K ARR.
4. **Worst-case scenario** (20% conversion, 4 pilots → 0 paid annuals): retain the pilot revenue ($48K), keep the field data, refine product-market fit. Y2 pivot informed by direct farmer feedback.

The risk isn't "30 sign up but only pilot." It's "0 pilot." Mitigation = field-day events + door-to-door priority farms = pre-qualified pipeline before the show.

## Pharma-model justification

JPL invoked pharma marketing: "blow brains out on marketing, providing it recoups fast."

For cultivOS Standard tier:
- Annual revenue per customer: ~$28K CAD (250 ac × $113 avg)
- Gross margin: ~55% (services-heavy mix)
- Annual gross profit: ~$15.4K
- CAC at $400 = **payback in 9.4 days of operating year** (15.4K ÷ 365 × 9.4 = $400)
- Even at $4,400 CAC (Y1 bootstrap mode): payback = ~104 days

This is the math that justifies aggressive Y1 spend.
